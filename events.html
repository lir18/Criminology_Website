Murder Mystery

Previous Next

The Murder Mysteries are one of our best events. Come dress up, act or be a detective in one of murders ! Each Murder Mystery is different, and who knows, you might be sitting just next to the murderer ! Our murder mysteries are themed, such as 1920s mobsters, Christmas, Wedding etc, so there will always one you will like to come to! The event starts at 7 P.M down at the Old College in a beautiful lounge area on a Friday night. Food and drinks are provided so you won't go hungry. In general, the Murder Mystery finishes at 9 PM and attendees are welcome to join us for some drinks in the town and party. You can either be one of the actors, i.e. one of the suspects (you might be the murderer!) and your entry is free ! Or you can come as one of the detectives. The detectives need to ask the suspects questions and find clues in order to find out who is the killer. Detectives need to contribute 2£ to the event. But don't worry, that money will be going for charity. In our last Murder Mystery, we raised over 40 pounds for the Marie Curie charity !

So not only will you be having an amazing time, but you will also be fed and contribute to charity ! What is there not to like? You can also invite people who are not part of the Society for the event, we don't mind.

Trips

The long awaited Prison Trip is finally here! Indeed, come join us on Friday the 23rd of March for an extraodinary experience ! Walk in the footsteps of prisoners who have been residents of the wings during the last 250 years. We shall be given a tour of the prison, and will be performing some activities. Not to mention that the prison will be giving us some ACTUAL prison uniforms, as we will try to break out from the Shrewsbury Prison! We will be leaving Aberystwyth at approximately 9:30 with two pick up locations with the student union mini bus (student Union and a location in town which would suit everyone).The prison tour lasts 2 hours, and there will be extra time allocated for people who want to go and have a meal, a bit of sight seeing or shopping etc. We should be back by 7 in Aberyswyth.

Go to the SU website, and click on the Criminology Society page here in order to get your 15£ tickets ! Be quick, as they sell quickly !

Films and Series

Come and watch a cool Criminology related film up at the picture house or down on the sea at Alexandra halls for a cosy night in. Get your popcorn and meet new people or chill out with your friends. Don't worry, there is no need to pay for this event ! Just come and chill out with us!

Sports

We might be an academic society, but don't you worry, if you like sport or want that extra motivation to go work out, we're here for you ! So far, the Criminology has participated in the annual Super Teams and we hope to take home first place next time ! The Society is also going to be participating in the Aber7! We are also going to be organising football and basketball sessions for those of you who just want to burn steam off and have a laugh. A lot of the members have a gym membership, so if you need a gym buddy, why not ask someone in the society to join you?

When the weather starts getting better, we are also hoping in doing regular hikes on the weekend, so that we can all stay fit, watch the beautiful Welsh landscape and take amazing photos. We will also be taking advantage of our wonderful beach and organise water gun fights when it gets really hot! We all know how hot Aberystwth can be in May!
