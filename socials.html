Get ready for an awesome fun time with the Aber Criminology Society

Be part of Aberystwyth's nightlife by joining the Criminology Society each Tuesday night for our awsome socials! Each week is a different theme, and if you don't want to spend much money, then you will have to get creative with the clothes you already have! All part of the fun ! We start each social at 9 PM on Tuesdays nights and start in Cambrina. We usualy move on to bars like rummers or BAR46 and usualy finsish the night in Harleys, Yokos and Pier. So, want to meet new people and have a great night? Join us !
