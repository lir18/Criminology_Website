Who are we?

We are the Official Society for the Criminology Society, after a long hiatus, the Criminology Society came back in 2017 and is here to stay! The 2017-2018 committee has done so much in order to kick start back the Society, you can go in our events page what we've been doing to give you a better idea of the amazing things we do !
